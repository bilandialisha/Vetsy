Love them, protect them, vaccinate them.

At Vetsy, we believe in proactive pet care. Vaccinations are a cornerstone of preventive medicine, providing your furry friend with the protection they need to live their best life.

More To Know

Dogs

  • Core vaccines: Protect against rabies, distemper, parvovirus, and adenovirus.
  • Non-core vaccines: Include vaccines against Bordetella (kennel cough), Leptospirosis, Lyme disease, and canine influenza.

Cats

  • Core vaccines: Guard against rabies, feline distemper (panleukopenia), feline calicivirus, and feline herpesvirus.
  • Non-core vaccines: Cover feline leukemia virus (FeLV), feline immunodeficiency virus (FIV), and feline infectious peritonitis (FIP) in high-risk environments.

Exotic

  • Vaccination needs vary widely depending on the species. Common vaccines for exotic pets include those for rabies, ferret distemper, avian influenza, and reptile-specific diseases.
